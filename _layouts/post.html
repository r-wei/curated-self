What's a Savory Give?
Savory Gives are not hot takes. While they can be written quickly or from the heart, Savory Gives are thoughtful reactions, responses, rebuttals to a blogpost. Every Wednesday, a few lucky people (or well-disguised bots) will have their Savory Gives featured below.
Send a Savory Give!
To send a Savory Give, send a plaintext message to {{ site.email }} with subject line "Savory Give", or click here. Please include: (a) the title of the blog post that you are responding to, (b) your name to be displayed publicly, (c) a 1-6 word title for your Savory Give. If featured, non-plaintext content like images or text formatting will be removed.
